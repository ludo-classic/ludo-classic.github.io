About Ludo Classic

Welcome to Ludo Classic, your number one source for playing India's favorite board game online! We are dedicated to providing you the very best of multiplayer gaming, with an emphasis on smooth real-time gameplay, user-friendly interface, and cross-platform compatibility.

Founded with a passion for classic board games, Ludo Classic brings the nostalgic joy of rolling the dice with friends and family straight to your screens. Whether you are sitting across the room or across the globe, our Firebase-powered real-time synchronization ensures you never miss a move.

Our Mission

Our mission is simple: to connect people through play. We believe that games like Ludo transcend generations and borders, offering a simple yet highly engaging way to spend quality time together. We have designed this platform to be lightweight, fast, and accessible directly from your browser without the need for heavy downloads.

We hope you enjoy playing Ludo Classic as much as we enjoyed building it for you. If you have any questions or comments, please don't hesitate to contact us.
